RustyEddy.com is a static website. There are no user accounts, no comments, no
checkout flow, and no sale of personal data.

## Analytics Cookies

This site uses Google Analytics only after you accept analytics cookies in the
cookie banner. Analytics helps me understand which pages are useful, what gets
read, and where broken or confusing pages may need attention.

Google Analytics may collect basic traffic information such as page views,
referring pages, browser type, approximate location, and device information. IP
anonymization is enabled in the site configuration.

## Declining Analytics

You can decline analytics cookies from the cookie banner. If you decline, the
site stores that choice in your browser and does not load Google Analytics.

You can also clear your browser's site data for `rustyeddy.com` to reset the
choice and show the banner again.

## External Links

Some pages link to external sites such as GitHub, LinkedIn, documentation, or
project repositories. Those sites have their own privacy policies and logging.
